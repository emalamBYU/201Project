My Typing Journey

How It All Started

I began learning to type as a way to improve my productivity and communication skills. At first, I struggled to find the right keys without looking, but with daily practice, my accuracy began to improve.

Progress Over Time

Through consistent practice sessions and using online typing tools, my speed has gone from a slow 25 words per minute to over 60 words per minute, and still climbing!

Typing Profile

You can view my detailed typing statistics and progress on my Monkeytype profile.

What's Next

My next objective is to achieve a peak typing speed of 200 words per minute and to consistently maintain an average speed between 160 and 180 words per minute.
