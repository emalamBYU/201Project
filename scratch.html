My Typing Journey

How It All Started

Typing is something that I first began learning on the iPad. Due to this, I struggled to type with propper form and wouldn't use my pinky fingers. I made it a goal to correct my typing habits to reduce strain while typing and to improve my overall speed and accuracy.

Progress Over Time

Through consistent practice sessions and using online typing tools, I have seen significant improvements in my typing speed and accuracy. I started with a peak speed of around 100-110 words per minute and have gradually increased it to an average of 140 words per minute with a peak of 188 words per minute. I can now reach these speeds much more consistently and without straining my fingers.

Typing Tools

Here are the steps I took to improve my typing, along with the tools I used at each stage:

  1. Set a typing goal
    • Improve accuracy
    • Increase speed gradually with accuracy
  2. Practice regularly
    • Monkeytype
    • Keybr
    • 10FastFingers
  3. Track progress
    • Keep a log
    • Review stats
    • Adjust goals based on performance
    • Focus on correcting improper keystrokes with websites like Keybr

Typing Profile

What's Next

My next objective is to achieve a peak typing speed of 200 words per minute and to consistently maintain an average speed between 160 and 180 words per minute.
